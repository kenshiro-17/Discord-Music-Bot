Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TC ("the Bot," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord music bot service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Bot, we automatically collect:

  • Discord User ID: Your unique Discord identifier
  • Discord Server ID: The ID of servers where the Bot is used
  • Voice Channel ID: The ID of voice channels the Bot joins
  • Text Channel ID: The ID of text channels where commands are used
  • Commands Used: The commands you execute and their parameters
  • Timestamps: When you interact with the Bot

2.2 User-Provided Information

You may provide:

  • Search Queries: Songs or artists you search for
  • URLs: YouTube or Spotify links you provide
  • Audio Files: Files you upload for playback

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Message content (except commands directed at the Bot)
  • Private or direct messages
  • Voice channel audio (beyond what's necessary for playback)
  • Personal information like name, email, or address
  • Payment information (the Bot is free)

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Primary Uses

We use collected information to:

  • Provide music playback functionality
  • Maintain song queues per server
  • Process your commands
  • Improve Bot performance and features
  • Debug errors and technical issues
  • Prevent abuse and spam

3.2 Analytics

We may use anonymized data to:

  • Track Bot usage statistics
  • Identify popular features
  • Monitor performance metrics
  • Improve user experience

4. Data Storage and Retention

4.1 Temporary Data

The following data is stored temporarily (in memory):

  • Music Queues: Deleted when queue ends or Bot leaves voice channel
  • Search Results: Deleted after 60 seconds
  • Uploaded Files: Deleted immediately after playback

4.2 Logs

Log files containing user IDs and commands are retained for:

  • Application logs: 14 days
  • Error logs: 30 days

4.3 Permanent Storage

We do NOT permanently store:

  • User profiles or preferences
  • Music history or playlists
  • Personal data

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Third-Party Services

We interact with these third-party services:

  • Discord: For bot functionality (subject to Discord's Privacy Policy)
  • YouTube: For music streaming (subject to YouTube's Privacy Policy)
  • Spotify: For music metadata (subject to Spotify's Privacy Policy)
  • Sentry (optional): For error tracking (anonymized data only)

5.2 No Selling of Data

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS)
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security updates

6.2 Limitations

While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Use the Bot at your own risk.

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Bot, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

8.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Stop using the Bot at any time by removing it from your server

8.2 Data Deletion

To request data deletion:

  • Most data is automatically deleted as described in Section 4
  • For log file removal, contact us with your User ID
  • We will process requests within 30 days

9. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot itself does not use cookies. Our website (if applicable) may use cookies for analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries different from your own. By using the Bot, you consent to international data transfers necessary for service provision.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on our website or Discord server. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
